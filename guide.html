Travel Guide

Plan your trip with ease.

How to Get There

By Bus

Take a Bachelor Express from Davao City Ecoland Bus Terminal directly to Mati City or other major towns.

By Van

Vans are available at the G-Mall Davao terminal (Mati bound) and are faster, but often more crowded. Travel time: 3-4 hours.

Getting Around

Local Transportation Options

Motorcycle (Habal-Habal)

Ideal for reaching remote destinations like Aliwagwag Falls or Mt. Hamiguitan trails. Agree on the fare beforehand.

Tricycle

Best for short distances within Mati City or other town centers.

Jeepney

The cheapest option for inter-town travel, but limited schedules and slower travel times.

What to Bring

Sun Protection

Sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses are essential for beach trips to Dahican or Cape San Agustin.

First Aid Kit

Include basic medicines, insect repellent, and motion sickness pills for long drives.
