The Mandaya People

Dagmay Weaving: The Living Art of the Mandaya People

Dagmay is the traditional hand-woven textile of the Mandaya indigenous people of Davao Oriental (especially in Caraga, Baganga, and Cateel). It is made from abaca fiber (locally called dagmay in Mandaya dialect), dyed with natural colors, and patterned with intricate geometric designs that carry deep spiritual and cultural meaning. Dagmay cloth. Their culture is rich in music, dance, and vibrant beadwork.
